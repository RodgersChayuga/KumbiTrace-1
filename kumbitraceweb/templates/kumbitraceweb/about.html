About KumbiTrace

Our Mission

At KumbiTrace, we are dedicated to reuniting missing persons with their loved ones. Our platform serves as a crucial link between families, communities, and law enforcement agencies in the search for missing individuals.

Who We Are

KumbiTrace is a team of passionate individuals committed to making a difference. Our diverse group includes technologists, social workers, and community advocates, all working together to create a safer society.

Our Approach

We leverage cutting-edge technology and community engagement to:

  • Provide a user-friendly platform for reporting and tracking missing persons cases
  • Facilitate quick dissemination of information to aid in searches
  • Collaborate with law enforcement and other agencies to maximize search efforts
  • Offer support and resources to families of missing persons

Join Our Cause

Every individual can make a difference. Whether you're reporting a case, sharing information, or volunteering your time, your contribution can help bring someone home. Join us in our mission to reunite families and strengthen communities.
